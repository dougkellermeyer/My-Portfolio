About Me

Passionate, driven software developer who enjoys building applications that solve problems, are easy to use, and hopefully make life a little easier.

Recruiting also helped further develop my soft skills, as I had to communicate on a daily basis with a wide range of both technical and non-technical people. It also opened my eyes to the world of software development. Software, like Chemistry, is made up of simple building blocks that can be combined to create new solutions to just about anything.

For the past 6-months I've been attending a full stack bootcamp at Case Western Reserve University in order to transition into software development, which is where I really want to be.
